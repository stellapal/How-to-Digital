How to Create Text with Solid Outline


Final Output


So you want to create a text with a solid outline on it. However, you found out that Canva does not have the solid outline option in their text effects selection. Is it still possible to make texts with the solid outline effect on Canva?

The answer is YES. It is still possible to create texts with the solid outline effect on Canva. However, you have to do some extra steps to achieve the desired result.

Follow these steps below to make a text with the solid outline effect on Canva.


Step #1: Add Text to Your Image


First, you need to add a text to your image. Just create a normal test. Do not put effects on the text yet.


Step #2: Duplicate the Text


Duplicate the text that you just created. You can do so by clicking the text, and pressing the "Ctrl + C" and "Ctrl + V" commands on your keyboard.

You can also click the "duplicate" option which you can find at the text options to create a copy of the text.


Step #3: Put the Hollow effect in the original copy


After you create a duplicate of the text, go back to the original copy and apply the Hollow effect on it.

You can do this step by clicking the "Effects" option around the text options menu. Then, choose the Hollow effect on the left side of your screen.

Remember to change the color of the original copy text. For this tutorial, I will use black for the solid outline effect.


Step #4: Change the color of the duplicate text and place it above the original copy


Once you are finished applying the Hollow effect on the original text, change the color of your duplicate text. The color of the texts should NOT be the same.

Then, place it above the original text just like the photo above.


Step #5: Send the duplicate copy backward


To finish the process, send the duplicate copy backward by pressing the right-click on your mouse and selecting the "Send Backward" option.
